The Tennessee Extension Master Gardener Program is a volunteer educational program that provides Tennesseans with horticulture education!
The Tennessee Extension Master Gardener Program trains and engages with volunteers to provide the public with research-based information on lawn and garden topics. TEMGs are volunteers who have a love of plants and horticulture coupled with Extension training to equip them to teach and assist Tennessee residents.
