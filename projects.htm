Mission:
The way station supports the Master Gardener Mission by promoting environmental stewardship. Monarch Waystations play a vital role in promoting environmental stewardship by restoring habitats, supporting biodiversity, educating communities, and engaging individuals in conservation action. It will serve as a tangible example of how small-scale efforts can make a meaningful difference in protecting the environment and its inhabitants.
