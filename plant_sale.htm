Spring Plant Sale

Every year, on the day preceding Mother's Day, the Master Gardeners of Robertson County host a plant sale. We kick off the process early in the year by nurturing a diverse range of plants in our greenhouse at Highland Rim. As Spring progresses, we tidy up our own gardens and pot any surplus plants to offer to the public during our sale.

The plant sale will be located at the UT Extension Office, 408 N. Main St, Springfield, TN

However starting in 2025, the location will transition to The Highland Rim Research and Education Center located at 3181 Experiment Station Rd, Springfield, TN 37172.

The proceeds generated from this event go towards supporting our community projects. It's a win-win situation: you get a great deal, and we reinvest the funds into our community.
