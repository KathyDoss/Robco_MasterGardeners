Robertson County Master Gardener Training

The Robertson County Master Gardener Training Class typically commences in February and comprises 14 sessions (one session per week). These sessions include both in-person classes held at the Highland Rim Research and Education Center, as well as educational materials that are accessible at your convenience from the Internet. The registration fee is $175 for individuals or $225 for two members of the same household who share training materials (this fee is subject to change). Here is a sample agenda:
  • Orientation/TEMG Program
  • Environmental Stewardship
  • Soils and Cover Crops
  • Botany
  • Landscape Design & Herbaceous Plants
  • Woody Ornamentals
  • Insect Pest & IPM
  • Pathology
  • Turf & Weed Management
  • Backyard Fruits
  • Vegetable Gardening
  • Pollinators / Wildlife

If interested in attending the next class, contact Bobary@UTK.edu.
